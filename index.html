About Me

I'm a software developer based in Singapore and a BSc in Computer Science at Singapore Management University. My passion for technology started at a young age when I was first introduced to the world of coding while tinkering with flash games. This sparked a love for programming that has grown and evolved over the years.

I have a strong interest in using technology to create meaningful and impactful solutions, and I am particularly drawn to the field of machine learning. I believe that technology has the power to change the world, and I am committed to using my knowledge and experience to contribute to this ongoing effort.
